About Us

Welcome to our Football Club! Established in 1882, we are dedicated to promoting the sport of football and fostering a sense of community among our players and fans. Our mission is to provide a platform for athletes to develop their skills, compete at the highest levels, and enjoy the beautiful game.

Our club is built on the values of teamwork, integrity, and excellence. We believe in nurturing talent from the grassroots level and providing opportunities for players of all ages and abilities. Whether you are a seasoned player or just starting out, our club offers a supportive and challenging environment to help you reach your full potential.

Club Details

  • Manager: Ange Postecoglou
  • Arena: Tottenham Hotspur stadium
  • Captain: Son Heung-min
  • Owner: Enic Group
  • Founder: september 5, 1882
  • Nicknames: spurs, the lilywhites
  • Location: Tottenham, london, United kingdom

Join us on our journey as we strive to achieve greatness on and off the field. Together, we can make a difference in our community and inspire the next generation of football stars.
